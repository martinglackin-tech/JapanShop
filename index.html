HINOYA

HINOYA is a legendary store in Ueno's Ameya Yokocho market, famous for its vast selection of high-quality Japanese denim and American casual wear. You can find iconic brands like Sugar Cane, Burgus Plus, and Buzz Rickson's. It's a must-visit for denim enthusiasts looking for authentic, durable pieces.

Kikuichi

With a history spanning over 750 years, Kikuichi began as a samurai sword smith. Today, they craft world-renowned kitchen knives. This is the place to find an exceptionally sharp and beautiful piece of Japanese craftsmanship, from santoku to gyuto knives. They also offer engraving services.

Details

Opening Hours

9:30 AM – 5:00 PM

Address

488 Zoshicho, Nara, 630-8211, Japan

Itoya Ginza

A paradise for stationery lovers, Itoya's flagship store in Ginza is a multi-story institution founded in 1904.
